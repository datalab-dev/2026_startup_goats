## General Information/Understanding

Teats are underneath the udders. What's looked at in the Booklet:
- Medial Suspensory Ligament (The cleft/septum looking thing)
- Teat Placement (Relative to Hocks/Knees; Slanted Sliding Scale) 
- Teat Diameter (Wideness)  

## Understanding of the Linear Appraisal System 

Basically 1-50. 

### Teat Placement:

_Position determined by the center of the teat, at the point where it attaches to the udder_

Outside Third (less than 25) < Two-Thirds the Way Out (>25) < One-Third the Way Out (>45)

### 

_Measured at Base_
_They also use this to understand the position of the teat from the udder & ease of milking_

1. 1/2" or less --> 5 or less 
2. 1-1/2" --> 25 or more 
3. 2-1/2" --> 45 or more

_1/2" differences give 10 pt differences_
